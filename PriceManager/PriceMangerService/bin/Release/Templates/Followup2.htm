To get your Parcel Solutions account to work for you and start saving you hours of time and money you will need to add "Custom Labels" to all your eBay listings if you haven't already.

Login to your eBay account, open your "Selling Manager Pro" if you cannot see the column "Custom label" click on the "Customise" link and check the "Custom labels" option.

If you can see the "Custom Labels" column then simply add a referring number or number letter combination that relates to the the item you are labeling (limited to 25 characters).

Keep track of these "Custom Labels" as you will have to weigh and measure each item and add them into your Parcel Solutions "Part Master"
