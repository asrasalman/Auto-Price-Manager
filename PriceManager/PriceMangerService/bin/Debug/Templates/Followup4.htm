For your Parcel Solutions account to work and have the ability to process multiple shipments (1-1000) with a few clicks, Parcel Solutions needs to know the weight, height, length and width of all your Ebay listings (eParcel Satchel rate clients need only to enter "weight")

Enter you eBay 'Custom Label" followed by the Description weight etc into the Part Master. If you have all you listings saved as a .CSV then you may load them all up with a few clicks!

You can edit and delete any listing in the Part Master as required.
