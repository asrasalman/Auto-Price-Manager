Processing large amounts of eBay shipments in minutes removes the stress and allows you to expand your range increasing profits expotentially.

Below is and check list to make sure your Parcel Solutions account is set to work for you.

1. Login to your Parcel Solutions account and click on "Ebay Pending Shipments" link to your eBay account!

2. Open "Profile" and fill in all relevant details (Be sure to add all your active eParcel charge codes)!

3. Add "Custom Labels" to all your eBay listings!

4. Weigh and measure all your listings and add them to the "Part Master" in Parcel Solutions!

5. Click on "Update Shipment Status" and learn how to get your processed Shipments tracking numbers back to your eBay account and automatically set them to "Sent"
